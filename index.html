What is School violence? Current Status, Causes, Consequences and Preventive Measures Against

Team G3

Welcome to explore the pressing issue of school violence. In this comprehensive article, we delve into the current state of school violence, examining its causes, trends, and far-reaching consequences for students, families, schools, and society. The purpose of our investigation is not only to shed light on the severity of the problem but also to suggest effective prevention measures.

Current State of School Violence

The current situation of school violence tends to increase and becomes extremely complicated. The problem of school violence has become painful news for the education sector and the entire society. School violence is currently becoming a hot spot worthy of attention for many parents, teachers and schools, and is a concern for the entire society.

The phenomenon of violence is not a new phenomenon, but recently this phenomenon has occurred more frequently in schools, revealing a more dangerous and serious nature. What is worrying is that the reasons leading to violence are sometimes very simple such as collisions while playing, on the way to school, conflicts and bad-mouthing each other on forums, social networks, etc.

According to the most recent data released by the Ministry of Education and Training, in one school year, there were nearly 1,600 incidents of students fighting inside and outside of school (about 5 incidents/day). ). There is one fight for every 5,200 students; For every more than 11,000 students, one was forced to drop out of school because of fighting; One in nine schools has students fighting. More worryingly, according to statistics from the Ministry of Public Security, each month there are more than 1,000 teenagers committing crimes. Previously: murderers between the ages of 30 and under 45 accounted for the highest number. Now it is down to 34% compared to 41% of the age group 18 to under 30 (the age group from 14 to under 18 accounts for 17%). Those data truly become a warning bell for families, schools and society to pay attention and take appropriate measures to combat this problem. Therefore, propaganda about the alarming situation of school violence is extremely necessary and practical.

What Is school violence?

Concept

School violence is rude, unruly behavior that disregards justice and morality, insults and oppresses others, causing mental and physical injuries that take place within school boundaries.

School violence includes acts of physical violence, including fights between students or physical punishments by the school; emotional violence, including verbal attacks; sexual violence, including rape and sexual harassment; forms of bullying among classmates; and bringing weapons to school

Causes Of School Violence

Causes of school violence can be divided into two main groups:

Reasons from individual students:

  • Aggressive personality, lack of control: Some students have an aggressive personality, lack control, get angry easily, quarrel often, like to express themselves, like to impose their will on others.
  • Lack of family attention and education: Some students lack family care and education, and are not taught about codes of conduct, ethics, and laws.
  • The impact of external factors: Students exposed to violent images and information from movies, books, games, toys, etc. can also lead to violent behavior.

The reasons come from family, school and society

  • Violence in the family: Violence in the family can affect children's psychology, causing them to learn to resolve conflicts with violence.
  • Lack of school management and supervision: Some schools do not really pay attention to moral education and life skills for students, leading to a situation where students lack conflict resolution skills, easily causing problems. quarrel, fight.
  • The impact of social factors: Rapid changes in society and the development of information technology can also have a negative impact on children's psychology, making them easily agitated and aggressive.

To prevent school violence, there needs to be close coordination between family, school and society. Families need to pay attention and educate their children about rules of conduct, ethics, and laws. Schools need to strengthen moral education and life skills for students, and strengthen student management and supervision. Society needs to strongly condemn and criticize acts of school violence, while creating a healthy and civilized living environment for children.

Consequences Of School Violence

Impact on students themselves:

  • Causes serious physical consequences.
  • What's worse is that many violent incidents have claimed the lives of innocent students, leaving behind disadvantages and pain not only physically but also mentally for students and their families.
  • Students who experience violence, especially mental and verbal violence, often feel hurt, depressed, anxious, lonely, depressed... Fear or phobia. This condition can even last a lifetime. They don't dare go out to play or go to school, and can't focus on studying.
  • Even children who only witnessed but did not participate in acts of violence were affected. Witnessing acts of violence makes children feel afraid, and if they see that those who commit violence are not punished, those who witness them may also follow the majority and support this behavior, and there will be many. the likelihood of becoming violent in the future.
  • The consequences that school violence causes, whether physical or mental, directly affect students' academic work as well as their future if not intervened in time.
  • With health effects along with anxiety, stress, and fear, students cannot study with the best possible results. Excessive psychological stress can even force students to end their studies, or it can also result in violent behavior that requires students to be expelled from school. From then on, their future took another not so positive turn.
  • In particular, children who commit violent acts and abuse of power from an early age may commit more criminal acts when they grow up than other children. Children involved in violent behavior, whether in one role or another, are at risk of abusing alcohol, tobacco, and drugs.

Impact on family:

  • The atmosphere and family life are disturbed, stressed, and worried.

Impact on school:

  • Violent acts not only negatively impact the victim but also make the school atmosphere heavy and tense with fear and insecurity always surrounding them.
  • In addition, students' acts of school violence will become the insecurity of parents when sending their children to school, losing the meaning of a strong and pure educational environment.

Impact on society:

  • Impact on traditional cultural features and valuable moral standards: Now there are students who blatantly argue with their teachers. Children argue with their parents.
  • Friends fight, happens quite often. These actions have further obscured the traditional cultural features of society, demonstrating an alarming moral decline and behavioral deviation.
  • Disrupt social order.

How To Prevent School Violence

With student:

  • Actively practice life skills, be obedient and polite with grandparents, parents, and teachers.
  • Obey school rules well
  • Stay away from violence. say no to violence
  • If you see a violent phenomenon, you must promptly report it to the school, teachers or competent authorities for timely intervention and handling.
  • Learn to control emotions
  • Actively participate in volunteer activities that the school organizes to increase the goodness and kindness in children.

For schools and educational management agencies:

  • Actively improve life skills training and introduce life skills teaching subjects into schools.
  • Organize schoolyard activities, volunteer activities that are good-natured and personality-oriented for students, helping students develop good qualities in themselves.
  • There are strict and appropriate punishments and education for students who commit violence, and there is timely support for victims of violence.
  • Organize propaganda about the harmful effects and ways to prevent school violence with teachers and students.
  • Coordinate with families and organizations located in the commune in the work of preventing school violence.

For teachers

  • Regularly pay attention, monitor and grasp the situation of students in the class you are in charge of or participating in teaching, especially homeroom teachers and teachers involved in teaching life skills.
  • Take timely educational measures to prevent the phenomenon of people at risk of violence against students in homeroom classes or participating in teaching.
  • Actively organize schoolyard activities, group activities during schoolyard activities or during activities, in order to increase the affection of students in the same class and school.
  • Create a bright, healthy learning and teaching environment.
  • Coordinate with families and schools to promptly pay attention and support students' difficulties and problems.

For family:

  • Parents need to create a healthy, loving living environment for their children
  • At the same time, coordinate closely with the school and homeroom teachers to promptly grasp your child's learning situation at school.

Support

To solve the problem of school violence, there needs to be close cooperation between families, schools, and educational management agencies. Here are some notes and support measures:

  • Parents need to regularly create a healthy and loving living environment for their children.
  • Provide maximum support to students in difficulty and coordinate with the school to resolve the situation.
  • Strengthen life skills education and include it in the curriculum.
  • Organize collective and volunteer activities to create a positive environment for students.
